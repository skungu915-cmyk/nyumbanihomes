5 Things to Check Before Renting in Nairobi
1️⃣
Water availability (not "available" — test it)
2️⃣
Security — guard, lighting, neighbourhood at night
3️⃣
Electricity — pre-paid or postpaid? Any arrears?
4️⃣
Parking — is it allocated or first-come-first-served?
5️⃣
Lease terms — get everything in writing
